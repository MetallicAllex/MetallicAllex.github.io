Meta资料缓存站说明

因为域名连接速度太慢，导致文件流传输卡顿，为了保证可以正常观看，故限制媒体流20kb/s的情况下将画质全部压缩为240P（即320x240，a风原画质为96x96）。

 

Because the connection speed of domain name is too slow, the file stream transmission is stuck. In order to ensure normal viewing, the image quality is compressed to 240p (320x240, the original image quality of "a wind" is 96x96) when the media stream is limited to 20kb/s .
